  • Privacy Policy

    1. Introduction

    TECHinfra is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure system.

    2. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information from you:

    • Personal information, such as your name, email address, and organization, when you create an account or submit data to the system.
    • Non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, when you interact with the system.
    • Usage data, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the actions you take within the system.

    3. How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Provide and improve the Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure system
    • Personalize your experience and tailor the system to your needs
    • Communicate with you about the system and any updates or changes
    • Analyze usage data to better understand how the system is being used
    • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
    4. Data Security and Retention

    TECHinfra takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

    5. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

    We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure system. We may also disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or respond to valid requests from law enforcement.

    6. Your Rights

    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, as well as the right to object to or restrict the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the information provided below.

    7. Changes to This Policy

    TECHinfra reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website or through the Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure system.

    8. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the handling of your data, please contact us at info@techinfra.com.
